# Health

## Standards

> Your non-negotiable health principles. These should be durable -- things that change quarterly at most. Claude uses these as guardrails when health-related topics come up.

- **Consistency over intensity.** Show up 4x/week, even if it's a light session. Streaks compound.
- **Sleep is the foundation.** Protect 7-8 hours. No screens after 10pm. If sleep breaks, everything else degrades.
- **Move every day.** Formal exercise 4x/week, but also walk, stretch, take the stairs. Sedentary time is the real enemy.
- **Don't optimize prematurely.** Get the basics right (sleep, movement, nutrition) before adding supplements, protocols, or biohacks.
